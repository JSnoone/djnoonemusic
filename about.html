About

Dj No One is a multi-instrumentalist musician, producer, and composer with a Degree in music production and a master's degree in Music Business. He has forged a dynamic career, collaborating with numerous studios in Bogota and creating tracks for TV shows, artists, and content creators spanning the globe. Beyond his proficiency as a drummer, guitarist, and pianist, Dj No One's academic excellence in music production positioned him as a standout student, enabling him to assist his mentors and instruct fellow peers. With nearly a decade of experience, his versatility encompasses a wide range of genres, including Pop, Dance Music, Latin styles, and Afrobeat. Notably, his collaborations extend to esteemed award-winning producers of Africa and Latin America, enriching his artistic journey and amplifying his prowess in the music industry.
